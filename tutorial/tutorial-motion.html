üëÄ Motion Detector Tutorial

Build a smart motion detector that lights up when movement is detected!

Step 1: Setup Your Workspace

Place your Arduino Uno and breadboard on a flat surface. Make sure you have all components ready.

Step 2: Insert PIR Motion Sensor

Carefully insert the PIR motion sensor module into the breadboard. The sensor has three pins: VCC, GND, and OUT.

Step 3: Connect Power Wires

Connect the VCC pin to Arduino 5V and GND pin to Arduino GND using jumper wires.

Step 4: Connect Signal Wire

Connect the OUT pin of the PIR sensor to digital pin 2 on the Arduino using a jumper wire.

Step 5: Add LED Circuit

Connect an LED and resistor to digital pin 13. Remember: LED long leg (anode) to resistor, short leg (cathode) to ground.

Step 6: Upload the Code

Open Arduino IDE, copy the motion detection code, and upload it to your Arduino board.

Step 7: Test Your Motion Detector

Power up your Arduino and wait 10-60 seconds for the PIR sensor to calibrate. Then wave your hand in front of it!

Your motion detector is working! The LED should light up whenever movement is detected. Try different distances and movements.
